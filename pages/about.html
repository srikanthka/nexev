About NexEV

Driving India's Electric Vehicle Revolution Since 2010

Pioneering EV Training

NexEV is a leading provider of electric vehicle components, manufacturing services, and comprehensive solutions for the rapidly growing EV industry in India. Founded with a vision to accelerate the adoption of clean transportation, we specialize in trading, importing, and distributing premium quality electric vehicle parts.

Our expertise spans across the entire EV ecosystem - from sourcing world-class components to providing assembly, testing, warehousing, and after-sales support. We serve dealers, distributors, garages, manufacturers, and end customers with equal commitment to quality and service excellence.

With strategic partnerships with leading manufacturers from Taiwan and other global EV hubs, NexEV ensures that our customers receive only authentic, certified products backed by comprehensive warranties and technical support.
