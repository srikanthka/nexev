Who We Are

Built on Engineering.
Driven by Purpose.

NexEV is a specialized electric mobility company solving India's EV reliability gap — sourcing BIS-certified components from trusted manufacturers in Taiwan, while building a rugged 250W stand-on scooter for real Indian conditions right here in Karnataka.

🇹🇼 Taiwan · Sourcing Office

The Taiwan Advantage

NexEV maintains a dedicated sourcing and technical operation in Taiwan — directly embedded within the global supply chain for EV components. Every BMS, charger, and controller is qualified at source for thermal stability, BIS compliance, and firmware integrity before procurement.

  • Direct access to Tier-1 BMS and charger manufacturers
  • Components validated at origin — not just on paper
  • First-look access to emerging battery cell technologies
🇮🇳 India · Karnataka

The India Execution

NexEV assembles its Gen-1 250W Stand-On Scooter in Karnataka using CKD components. The focus is repairability and performance-per-rupee — built to survive 45°C heat and monsoon flooding, not just pass a lab test. Service-First distribution from day one.

  • CKD assembly in Bengaluru, Karnataka
  • IP67 connectors, all-terrain wheels, 120mm ground clearance
  • Local warranty and authorized service network

Sourcing & Validation

We don't just buy parts — we qualify them. Working directly within Taiwan's component supply chain, every BIS-certified BMS and charger is vetted for thermal stability, firmware integrity, and long-term reliability.

Service & Distribution

An authorized Service-First network in India — local warranty coverage and direct technical support. Dealers get territory rights, training, and a supply chain built for uptime, not just initial sale.

Engineered Products

The 250W Stand-On Scooter is Phase 1 of a roadmap toward proprietary IP in motor control, BMS firmware, and robotics. Each phase generates revenue and deepens capability before the next begins.
