BIS Compliance — What It Means for Suppliers

  • Battery cells/packs: BIS IS 16046 — mandatory for commercial sale
  • BMS: BIS IS 16893 — required for distribution
  • Chargers: BIS IS 16170 — compulsory registration
  • Controllers: BIS IS 17017 — mandatory for EV use
  • NexEV can support the BIS registration process for partner products
